Lasagna

Ingredients

  • dried lasagna pasta
  • ground beef
  • onion
  • crushed garlic
  • canned tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  3. Mix them together.
  4. Et voila.
